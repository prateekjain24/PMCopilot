## Job Map

The job map breaks the core functional job into 8 universal steps. For each step, identify what the performer does, where they experience pain, and what needs are unmet.

### Step 1: Define

*Determine what needs to be done and plan the approach.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Identify which competitors to analyze and what aspects to focus on (features, pricing, UX, positioning)."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Unclear criteria for selecting which competitors matter most"]
- [e.g., "Scope creep -- analysis expands beyond what is actionable"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "A structured framework for scoping competitive research based on strategic priorities"]

---

### Step 2: Locate

*Gather the inputs, materials, and information needed to do the job.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Find competitor apps, websites, pricing pages, review sites, and analyst reports."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Information is scattered across dozens of sources"]
- [e.g., "Difficult to access competitor apps without creating accounts or obtaining APKs"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "A single starting point that aggregates competitive data sources"]

---

### Step 3: Prepare

*Set up the environment and organize resources before executing.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Create a spreadsheet or document to capture findings; set up test accounts on competitor platforms."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "No standardized template; every analysis starts from scratch"]
- [e.g., "Setting up competitor accounts is time-consuming and sometimes requires fake information"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "Ready-to-use templates and automated environment setup for competitor evaluation"]

---

### Step 4: Confirm

*Verify readiness and ensure everything is in place before proceeding.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Validate that all competitors in scope are accessible and that the research plan covers the right dimensions."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Discovering mid-analysis that a key competitor has changed their product or pricing"]
- [e.g., "No way to verify completeness of the research scope"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "A checklist or validation step that confirms research readiness before execution begins"]

---

### Step 5: Execute

*Perform the core activity of the job.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Navigate competitor apps and websites, take screenshots, document features, test workflows, and note UX patterns."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Extremely time-consuming -- a single competitor can take 4-8 hours to fully evaluate"]
- [e.g., "Easy to miss screens or features during manual navigation"]
- [e.g., "Screenshots become disorganized quickly"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "Automated navigation and screenshot capture that ensures comprehensive coverage"]
- [e.g., "Structured output that organizes findings by feature area"]

---

### Step 6: Monitor

*Track progress and check whether the job is being done successfully.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Review collected data to check for gaps, verify accuracy, and assess whether enough depth has been achieved."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Hard to know when 'enough' research has been done"]
- [e.g., "No progress tracking -- analysis feels open-ended"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "Coverage indicators that show which areas have been analyzed and which remain"]

---

### Step 7: Modify

*Make adjustments and handle exceptions or changes.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Update findings when competitors launch new features; adjust analysis scope based on stakeholder feedback."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Competitive analysis becomes stale quickly -- no easy way to detect changes"]
- [e.g., "Rework is manual and repetitive"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "Change detection that alerts when competitors update their products"]
- [e.g., "Incremental updates instead of full re-analysis"]

---

### Step 8: Conclude

*Finalize, share results, and wrap up the job.*

**What the performer does at this step:**
[e.g., "Synthesize findings into a report or presentation; share with stakeholders; archive for future reference."]

**Pain points:**
- [e.g., "Synthesis is the hardest part -- turning raw observations into strategic recommendations takes significant effort"]
- [e.g., "Reports are read once and then forgotten; no living document"]

**Unmet needs:**
- [e.g., "Automated synthesis that produces presentation-ready competitive comparisons"]
- [e.g., "A persistent, searchable competitive knowledge base"]
