## Review Cadence

- **Weekly:** Check status of "Now" initiatives. Flag items that are at risk.
- **Monthly:** Review "Next" items. Promote to "Now" if ready; demote or remove if assumptions have changed.
- **Quarterly:** Full roadmap review. Evaluate "Later" items against updated strategy. Archive completed initiatives. Add new items from backlog and stakeholder requests.

## Tips for Maintaining This Roadmap

1. **Resist the urge to over-fill "Now."** If everything is "Now", nothing is prioritized. Limit "Now" to what can realistically be delivered this quarter given actual team capacity.
2. **"Later" is not a parking lot.** Items in "Later" should have genuine strategic intent. Use a separate backlog for ideas that are not yet roadmap-worthy.
3. **Update commitment states honestly.** If a "Now" item is at risk, surface it early rather than silently slipping it to "Next."
4. **Use this roadmap as a communication tool.** Share it broadly. The value is in alignment, not in the document itself.
5. **Pair with prioritization frameworks.** Use RICE, ICE, or Cost of Delay scores to justify sequencing decisions and resolve debates.
