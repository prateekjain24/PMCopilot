**Q1: [What is the estimated cost to build and operate this?]**

[Answer: Break down development cost, infrastructure cost, and ongoing operational cost.]

**Q2: [What is the revenue model and projected financial impact?]**

[Answer: Describe how this product generates or protects revenue. Include projections if available.]

**Q3: [What are the key technical risks?]**

[Answer: Be honest about technical challenges and your plan to address them.]

**Q4: [What are the dependencies on other teams?]**

[Answer: List every cross-team dependency, what is needed, and the current status of each.]
